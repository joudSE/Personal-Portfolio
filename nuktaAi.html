NuktaAI - Smart Article Summarizer

Turn long articles into clear insights — instantly

Project Overview

NuktaAI is a smart browser extension designed to simplify the way people read online content. It uses Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to analyze long articles and highlight the most important information instantly. The goal is to enhance productivity by reducing reading time while improving understanding.
